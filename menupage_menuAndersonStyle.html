FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q. What does fashion have to do with director Wes Anderson?
A. Director Wes Anderson is so fashionable that he wears suits on set in the desert. In particular, the suede brown suit and velvet purple suit have become his signature suit, making it easy to find people wearing various suits and wandering around the set, party, and red carpet. Sometimes you can see them dressed up in clacky, retro suits. Like this, he creates a character by putting his unique style into each character in the movie. Wes Anderson, who has this stylish sense, is already famous not only in movies but also in the fashion industry. World-class brands such as Gucci, Prada, and Miu Miu were inspired by him and presented various shows. In addition, he has shown his essence in fashion films taken with various fashion brands.
